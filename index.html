Field notes

Go out smarter

01

Arrive on the early side

The best rooms fill fast and close their doors. Getting in an hour early usually means no queue and a better spot on the floor.

02

Read the room's rules

No-phone floors and one-in-one-out policies exist for a reason. Respect them and the night stays good for everyone.

03

Plan the way home first

Know your last train or a night bus before you go in. A sorted exit means you can actually stay until the good part.
